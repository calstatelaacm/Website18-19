How to pay using Google Pay?


Step 1:

Create a Gmail account if you don't have one to pay using Google Pay. Use this link to sign into Google Pay or create an account:     Google Pay

Step 2:

  • Click on "Send or request money" and enter the information as below (Note: Your amount may vary between $10 and $5 depending on which membership you choose. For a list of memberships and benefits click here)
