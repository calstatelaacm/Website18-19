Our Student Chapter of ACM

Benefits of ACM:

Skills: You will learn and develop various skills including hard skills like programming by working on various projects throughout the year and some soft skills like communicating and working in a team
Latest news: You will be up-to-date with the latest news in tech and other events like Hackathons
Networking: You will meet with other fellow CS Cal State LA students and work on different ideas
Projects: Members will be introduced to various coding projects of different levels giving them an opportunity to apply concepts they learn in class to hands-on coding experiences.
